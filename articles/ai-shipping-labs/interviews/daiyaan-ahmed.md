# Interview: Daiyaan Ahmed

## Persona

Alex - the engineer transitioning to AI. Daiyaan is a data lead at a fintech startup, originally a Data Engineering Zoomcamp graduate. He learns through articles and videos but struggles to find time to build, and works best with accountability, mentorship, and community[^1].

See [personas.md](../personas.md) for full persona definitions.

## Initial Response

Daiyaan introduced himself with a thank-you for Data Engineering Zoomcamp - it turned him from "a casual office worker into a genuinely passionate engineer". The way things were explained made everything click without feeling like a black box, and he could build with confidence[^1].

He is now a data lead at a fintech startup and is trying to grow into AI. He keeps learning through articles and videos but struggles to find time to build. He has realised he works best with accountability, mentorship, and community[^1].

He missed the AI Engineering Buildcamp deadline, which is why he wanted to try AI Shipping Labs[^1].
