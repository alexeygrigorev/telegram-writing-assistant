# How to Use Your Plan

This is a short note to share with each AI Shipping Labs member alongside their personalised plan. It explains where the plan came from and how to work with it.

## What the Plan Is Based On

The plan we prepared for you is built on three inputs[^1]:

- Your own answers from the intake and interview
- Alexey's comments and recommendations
- Internal review on our side before sending

We went through it together and it makes sense to us, but you are the one who needs to use it.

## Your First Step

Look at the plan and decide for yourself whether it fits[^1]:

- Does it suit you specifically?
- Ask questions if something is unclear
- Or change it right away and adjust it to your situation

The plan is only a suggestion - a starting point so we have something concrete to begin from. It is not a contract.

## The Plan Is Flexible

If you deviate from the plan, that is fine[^1]. As you go through the sprint you will discover things in real time:

- Some weeks have a sticking point and need more time
- Some weeks go faster than expected
- Some steps will not work and need to be replaced

Treat the plan as a guideline, not a strict schedule. We will adjust together as the sprint progresses.
