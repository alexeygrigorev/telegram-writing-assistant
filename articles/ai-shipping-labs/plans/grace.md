# Plan: Grace

## AI Shipping Labs Personal Plan Intake

### 1) Your Initial Input

I'm a Stockholm based iOS engineer with 7 yrs experience. My goals are unclear at this point, I'd like to elevate in my current position but also position myself to change jobs to a more ML/AI role. Maybe this will be clear as time goes on.

### 2) Questions

#### What feels most important for you right now: growing in your current iOS role, moving toward ML/AI, or getting clarity before choosing a direction?

Getting clarity at this time. I'd be happy to find a way to integrate ML/AI in my iOS development and this would help me pivot to ML/AI engineer.

#### What do you feel is currently missing between your profile today and the kind of role or work you may want next?

Projects that show that I can use ML/AI in my iOS career as well as that I can do ML/AI on its own.

#### What have you already done, tried, or learned that could be relevant for this transition?

I use coding assistants in my day to day when necessary, specifically GitHub Copilot CLI which is company approved and Claude. My experience with software development is a big plus and so I'd like to leverage it.

#### What do you hope to achieve with this plan in the next 6 to 8 weeks? If you had to choose one concrete outcome for the next 6 weeks, what should it be?

Being able to build an end-to-end AI solution.

#### How much time can you realistically commit each week over the next 6 to 8 weeks?

1.5 hrs Mon-Fri. More on the weekends.

#### What strengths from your background should the plan build on?

My SDLC experience, I'm happy to delve deeper into a concept and try to figure it out. I'd suggest that the plan shouldn't be too beginner. If you introduce a concept, you can give me topics I can dive into deeper that are related.

#### What is blocking you most right now from moving forward?

Information paralysis. It's hard to know which way to go when I don't even have the basics yet. A bit of background here; I learnt how to code through a coding bootcamp before that I had tried to do it myself but there was too much information out there. I got the basics in the bootcamp, and from there I pivoted to iOS development which wasn't even taught at the bootcamp.

#### What type of accountability would be most effective for you?

Sprints and having an accountability partner.

#### What would make you feel that, at the end of the next 6 to 8 weeks, the plan was worthwhile?

If I can take a project and build an end-to-end AI solution; discovery, modeling, evaluation, deployment, iteration.

## Alexey's Recommendations

Grace just signed up for the AI Engineering Buildcamp course. Since she has access to the course, her main task for the next weeks is to go through the course and pick a project she wants to build[^3].

The recommendation is to focus from the very beginning on the project she wants to make. Based on that, it is easier to decide what else to do.

The course already has very concrete steps laid out. The plan we prepare should help her combine the course material with preparing her own project over the remaining 7 weeks of the course. The course itself is 9 weeks long, the first week is already done, so 8 weeks remain - 7 weeks for the plan, with the final week for polishing the project.

The project already has some guidance inside the course, but adding a little extra structure for her may be useful.

She should also participate in the sync-ups.
