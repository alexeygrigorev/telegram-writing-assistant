# DataTalks.Club SEO Growth

DataTalks.Club has been working on SEO and seeing meaningful growth in organic traffic from Google Search.

## Traffic Milestone

In January 2026, the site reached 9,000 clicks from Google Search in a 28-day period, as tracked by Google Search Console[^1].

This milestone represents the cumulative effect of consistent content creation and SEO optimization over time.

## Implications

Growing organic search traffic indicates that:
- Content is ranking well for relevant search terms
- The site is building authority in its domain
- More people are discovering courses and resources through search
- Less reliance on paid promotion or social media for discovery
