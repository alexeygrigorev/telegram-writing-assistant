Why Something New?

How It Came Together

  1. AI Engineering Buildcamp on Maven - students wanted more structure and depth
  2. Personal newsletter - a space to share daily learning
  3. A smaller group to go deeper - live sessions, solving problems together


It came together naturally into a community.

DataTalks.Club Isn't Going Away

All Zoomcamps continue. Free education stays free.

This Is Not a Replacement

Just like the Maven Buildcamp didn't replace LLM Zoomcamp.


AI Shipping Labs is for people who want closer connection and the kind of support we can't sustainably provide as part of a free community.
